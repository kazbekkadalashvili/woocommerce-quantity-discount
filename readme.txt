== Description ==
This plugin allows you to add quantity discounts to your WooCommerce products. You can specify a quantity and a discount percentage for each discount tier. When a customer adds a product to their cart with a quantity that meets or exceeds a discount tier, they will receive the discount.

To use the plugin, simply go to the \"Products\" page in your WordPress dashboard and edit the product you want to add a quantity discount to. In the \"Product Data\" tab, scroll down to the \"Quantity Discounts\" section and enter the quantity and discount percentage for each discount tier.

For example, to offer a 10% discount on orders of 10 or more items, you would enter \"10\" in the \"Quantity\" field and \"10\" in the \"Discount Percentage\" field.

Once you have added your quantity discounts, save the product and they will be applied to the product in the cart.

This plugin is free to use and is fully compatible with WooCommerce 3.0 and later.

== Installation ==
1. Download the plugin ZIP file.
2. In your WordPress dashboard, go to \"Plugins\" -> \"Add New\".
3. Click on the \"Upload Plugin\" button and select the ZIP file you downloaded.
4. Click \"Install Now\" and then activate the plugin.
